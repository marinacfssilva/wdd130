Sao Paulo

Sao Paulo, founded in 25 January 1554, is the most populous city in Brazil and one of the largest in the world, with more than 12 million inhabitants. It is located in the State that bears the same name, which is located in the Southeast region of the country. The city of Sao Paulo is the country's main economic, cultural and financial center, and is characterized by numerous residential and commercial buildings. The city is located within the Atlantic Forest biome, but much of the native vegetation was deforested in the urbanization process. The Tiete and Pinheiros rivers are the two main watercourses that flow through the city, however both had their course intensely modified to meet the demands of the urbanization process and currently both are highly polluted.São Paulo is the most multicultural city in Brazil and one of the most diverse in the world, it is the city with the largest populations of Italian, Portuguese, Japanese, Spanish, Lebanese and Arab ethnic origin outside their respective countries.
